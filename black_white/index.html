About Hofstra Rugby

Hofstra Men's Rugby was established in 1935, and competes in the powerhouse Tri-State Conference. In the Fall of 2019 Hofstra went 7-1, making them Tri-State Conference Champions. After 2nd place finish in the Northeast in the 2019 National Small College Organization, Hofstra was ranked 20th in the country. This ranking is in Divison 3 Rugby (NSCRO), having over 240 teams in it's union.

Hofstra University is a private institution founded in 1935 in Hempstead, NY and is about 25 miles east of NYC.

Hofstra Rugby prides itself in community service, having volunteered at organizations such as HopeFitness, The American Red Cross, and homes with disabled children and adults.
